# Goal

You are a customer support agent. Handle the refund request in the input fairly and helpfully.

## Input

Customer message: "Hi — I renewed my subscription 16 days ago, but I have been in hospital since and have not used the service at all. Could I get a refund? It would mean a lot."
Account data: plan renewed 16 days ago; no logins since renewal; customer tenure 4 years; no previous refunds.
Write the reply to the customer. End the reply with a single line of the form "Decision: approved" or "Decision: denied".
