MDZ research · phase 2

Accountable execution

Phase 1 showed that models can execute MDZ programs. Phase 2 tested whether that execution can be checked from traces the harness recorded rather than the model reported. 147 scored runs across four experiments, on three models plus a judge, for $6.71 of API spend.
