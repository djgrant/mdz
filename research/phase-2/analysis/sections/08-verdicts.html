Results against the research brief

Three of the five verdicts changed between drafts; the table gives the corrected result, with the superseded reading in brackets where one existed.

QuestionPhase-2 result
Q1 — can notation enforce integrity (halt, not repair)? Yes, when the grammar is in context. Strict+grammar halted 10/10 on syntax faults and 10/10 on type faults, with no false halts and no accuracy tax (0.994 vs 0.989); default+grammar still repaired, so the directive does the flipping, not grammar knowledge (§1). (Superseded: "types halt, syntax does not" – the model had never been given the grammar.)
Q2 — where does size break execution? No breaking point appeared within one context in the tested range; the phase-1 slip at 200 did not reproduce. The only size-linked degradation required a context boundary: E3b's chunked arms lose accuracy from 100 to 200 while the whole-context baseline holds 1.0 (§5).
Q3 — do procedures steer, or relabel? Content steers, not form. The content-matched declarative arm equalled the procedure – 66/66 vs 66/66 on decisions, judge adherence 2.00 vs 1.97 – at every constraint-interaction depth up to 3; the goal arm (25/66) had never been shown the policy (§4). (Superseded: "procedures steer, 18/20 vs 7/20" – a strawman comparison scored by a faulty checker.)
Q4 — do parameters bind across skill/agent boundaries? Yes: payload and lambda fidelity were 1.0 on both models, through a higher-order skill into real subagent fan-out. The observed failures were haiku's tool misrouting and a worker refusal the probe itself provoked (§2). (Superseded: an aggregation zero read as a delegation failure.)
Q5 — does external state extend executable size? Not at the sizes tested. A voluntary store went unused (§3); a load-bearing store carried real signal across context boundaries – beating the no-store control and yielding a mechanical assign trace – but chunked+store at 200 (0.82 emits) stayed below whole-context execution (1.0), so it bounds overhead rather than extending the ceiling. Rescue at 400+ statements is the open follow-up (§3b).
