Cross-cutting findings

Phase 3 asked where procedural form earns its keep. The four experiments give one coherent answer with an uncomfortable rider.

  • Form pays exactly where the state-reconstruction bet said it would — and nowhere else. E1's procedure advantage exists only when the event log is withheld (sonnet fx: 0.708 vs 0.583 resumption; log: 1.0 vs 1.0), and E3's store only pays past the whole-context cliff (800 statements: 0.975 vs 0.537; at 400: a wash). Position and external state are worth carrying when, and only when, the model cannot reconstruct or retain the state itself.
  • Capability dominates form, three ways. Sonnet with prose rules out-resumes haiku with the procedure on every E1 metric. A full event log erases sonnet's form gap entirely. And in E2b, sonnet was the arm that refused to execute the skill's shape — all six sonnet skill runs truncated the loop to one fan-out and skipped the judge, while haiku followed the notation. Procedural form binds weak models more than strong ones, in both the helpful and the unhelpful direction.
  • For weak models, form buys a failure mode, not success. E1 haiku fails either way, but the procedure yields repeat-and-finish (0.667 repeat, 0.688 completion) where the rules yield skip-and-abandon (0.210 skip, 0.479 completion). Which failure is safer depends on whether the repeated side effects are idempotent — a design lever, not a free win.
  • The uncomfortable rider: iteration beat divergence-and-selection on both selection regimes. The ralph loop — no kept alternatives, no reduce, no judge — won E2a by two orders of magnitude (median ~102× vs 3.0×) and took E2b's judge 9–3 against the skill. The map-reduce skill's fan-out is faithful and consistently beats its content-matched prose twin (which never fanned out at all), so the notation delivers its mechanism; the mechanism just lost. For tasks with compounding structure, best-of-N single shots is the wrong shape, and three fresh contexts over a shared workspace is itself a form of external state — the working directory is the store.
  • The E2 arms rank the same under a benchmark and a judge. ralph > goal ≈ skill on both, which is some evidence the E2b judge is tracking quality rather than style — the objective regime agrees with it.
